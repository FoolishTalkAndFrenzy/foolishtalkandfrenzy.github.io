About Us

Hosts

Matt

Matt is a first year student at the University of Minnesota studying tuba performance. He was born and raised in Minneapolis, Minnesota. When he’s not in class, he enjoys hanging out with his friends and making the homies laugh. Additionally, Matt can often be found working events at Ted Mann Concert Hall or making drinks at Caribou Coffee. He’s thrilled to be working on this podcast and is overjoyed that you decided to check it out!

Darby

After spending most of her childhood in Papillion, Nebraska, Darby is delighted to reside in the big city as a first-year student at the University of Minnesota (Twin Cities). You can oftentimes find her giving tours of the campus as part of her job in the Visit Office. Darby is enthusiastic about a number of activities, including, but not limited to, quilting, baking bread, wearing funky jackets, reading War and Peace, visiting art museums, being theatrical, and of course, recording this podcast with her dear friend Matt. She has been described as “whimsical” and “flamboyant”.
